Dad's Favorite Peanut Butter Cookies

A peanut butter cookie is a type of cookie that is distinguished for having peanut butter as a principal ingredient. The cookie originated in the United States, its development dating back to the 1910s. My father's peanut butter cookie will make your drool in as you dig into pure heaven!

  • ½ cup shortening
  • ½ cup crunchy peanut butter
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour, plus more as needed
  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
